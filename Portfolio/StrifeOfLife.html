Strife of Life

Strife of Life is a game about the creative minds of our childhood and the fight against our daily tasks. As children many of us likely thought about a triangle ruler as a pistol or make slingshot with it by attaching rubber bands to the corners. This game takes the struggles of the every day life and turns them into a playful fight. Visualizing the fight we keep up all the time using whatever is around us.
In a group of three we developed the game within or 3rd semester.

Trailer


The core idea of the game came from a friend of mine and I took it and developed it further(I asked him for allowance, don't worry).
During the project my job was to coordinate, game design and code the inventory with the crafting system. The rooms need to be distributed equally since there are different classes, with different rooms they can access. No class is supposed to be superior, instead they have different options, stengths and weaknesses.
The crafting system was fun to create, as foundation I had to make an inventory that is flexible, accessible and is correctly displayed. After that was done, the crafting itsself needed to check up availablities with the inventory and remove or add items. To make that process editable easily I used presets that defined what kind of and how many items one needed to craft an item.


Sadly my whole idea was way too ambitious for a project in the thrid semester. The mindmap is gigantic and my passion for this game is burning.
It's still my dream to make this game reality!
