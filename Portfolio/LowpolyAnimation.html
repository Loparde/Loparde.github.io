Lowpoly Campfire Animation


Relax and sit back while you enjoy your camping trip with scary stories around the campfire. But don't forget to prepare some food!

The Animation Video

The video consists of three parts. The grocery and equipment shopping in the early morning fog. The afternoon at the lake fishing for some dinner. And the evening around the campfire with some horror stories to entertain the night.
My contribution to this project was the idea, the scenery and animation of the first and third scene.
